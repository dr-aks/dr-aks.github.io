Research Enquiries

Prospective Members

Motivated candidates interested in PhD positions are encouraged to write with a statement of research interest, CV, and transcripts. Candidates holding JRF, GATE, or UGC-NET qualifications are particularly welcome.

Postdoctoral applicants with NPDF, Raman Fellowship, or SERB funding are invited to discuss potential alignment with ongoing projects in thermoelectric materials, GaN-based devices, and EUV/X-ray optics.

M.Sc./M.Tech project students should contact at least one semester in advance. Project topics are assigned based on availability and background.

ISRO RESPOND Project — EUV/X-ray Multilayer Mirror Prototypes for Space Instrumentation
The project aims to develop nanomaterial-based multilayer mirror prototypes for future EUV and X-ray polarimetric space missions funded by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). The fellow will work on thin film deposition, optical characterisation, and structural analysis of multilayer coatings.
JRF: ₹31,000/month + HRA · SRF: ₹35,000/month + HRA (as per DST norms) Duration: Up to project completion Central University of Jammu
Eligibility

  • Valid NET/JRF qualification (CSIR-UGC or equivalent) preferred; GATE score acceptable for SRF
  • Background in thin film physics, optics, or X-ray/EUV instrumentation is an advantage

DRDO CARS Project — Radiation Effects in GaN/AlGaN HEMT Devices

IUAC UFR Project — Defect Evolution in Wide-Bandgap Semiconductors under Swift Heavy Ion Irradiation

Application Process

How to Apply

Send an Enquiry Email

Write to ashish.phy@cujammu.ac.in with subject line "Application — [Position Name]". Include a brief statement of research interest and your CV.

What to Include

Full academic CV, mark sheets (UG and PG), qualification test certificates (JRF/NET/GATE), and a short paragraph describing your research interests and why you want to join this group.

Shortlisting & Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be called for a research discussion (in-person or online). The discussion will focus on physics fundamentals and experimental methodology relevant to the project area.

Formal Enrolment

JRF/SRF positions follow SERB/DST/IUAC guidelines. PhD scholars enrol through the Central University of Jammu PhD admission process. Candidates are encouraged to apply to CUJ PhD programmes independently during open admission cycles.

Interested in joining the lab?

We welcome applications year-round for PhD and project positions. Motivated students with a genuine interest in experimental physics are encouraged to reach out early — even before a formal opening is advertised.

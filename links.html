Research Funding Schemes
Grants for Physics faculty, principal investigators, postdocs & doctoral students in India

ANRF Core Research Grant (CRG)

Main extramural funding for individual researchers or groups at any Indian institution; no fixed upper budget; 3-year tenure. Flagship scheme of Anusandhan National Research Foundation.

Faculty

SERB Start-up Research Grant (SRG)

Two-year seed grant of ₹30 lakh for early-career faculty (within 5 years of first regular appointment) to establish an independent research programme.

Faculty

ANRF Advanced Research Grant (ARG)

Competitive grant for high-impact frontier research by established groups with a strong publication record; larger budgets for ambitious proposals.

Faculty

SERB MATRICS

Fixed grant of ₹2 lakh p.a. + overheads tailored for active mathematicians and theoretical physicists with a strong credentials and publication record.

Faculty

SERB-POWER Grant

Promoting Opportunities for Women in Exploratory Research — supports women scientists at all career stages with dedicated research grants and fellowships.

Faculty

SERB National Post Doctoral Fellowship (N-PDF)

₹80,000/month + HRA for PhD holders within 5 years of viva; 2-year fellowship at any recognised Indian institution. One of the most competitive postdoc awards in India.

Postdoc

SERB Ramanujan Fellowship

For brilliant Indian scientists returning from abroad; ₹1,35,000/month + research grant for 5 years. Age limit: below 40 years. Helps reintegrate into India's research ecosystem.

FacultyPostdoc

CSIR–Nehru Science Postdoctoral Fellowship

₹50,000/month + HRA for fresh PhD holders in CSIR labs; 2-year fellowship (extendable to 3 for outstanding work). Covers sciences including Physics and Materials Science.

Postdoc

CSIR–Raman Research Fellowship (RRF)

For CSIR scientists to conduct collaborative research at premier foreign institutions; USD 2,500/month + travel; duration 2–6 months. Stimulates international exposure.

FacultyPostdocInternational

Raman–Charpak Fellowship (Indo-French)

DST–CEFIPRA exchange programme for Indian PhD students & postdocs to undertake 3–6 month research visits at French laboratories in any area of science.

PhDPostdocInternational

CSIR NET JRF — Physical Sciences

Gateway to PhD fellowship in India: ₹31,000–35,000/month + ₹20,000 p.a. contingency; conducted twice a year by NTA. Mandatory qualification for most funded PhDs.

PhD

UGC Schemes Portal

All UGC-funded schemes: Major/Minor Research Projects, STRIDE, BSR fellowships, Junior Research Fellowships for university faculty & their PhD students.

FacultyPhD

DST Fellowship Opportunities — Master List

DST's consolidated portal listing all national fellowships, career-development awards, and women scientist schemes. Best starting point for any Indian researcher exploring funding.

FacultyPostdocPhD
Fellowships & Internships
For school, UG, PG & PhD students and college teachers in Physics & Materials Science

DST-INSPIRE Fellowship & Scholarship

Scholarship for top BSc/MSc students in Natural Sciences: ₹80,000/year + ₹20,000 summer attachment grant. Also offers INSPIRE Faculty Award and PhD Fellowship.

UG/PGPhDFaculty

INSPIRE–MANAK (Innovation Award)

DST scheme for school students (Class 6–10) to submit innovative ideas; ₹10,000 award to selected entries; national exhibition with mentorship at premier institutions.

School

Science Academies' SRFP (IAS / INSA / NASI)

Joint Summer Research Fellowship Programme by India's three national science academies; 2-month placement with a Fellow of the Academy; open to students AND college teachers.

UG/PGTeacher

TIFR Visiting Students Research Programme (VSRP)

Summer research at TIFR Mumbai in Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science, Astronomy; for final-year UG/PG students; stipend and accommodation provided.

UG/PG

PRL Summer Internship Programme

8-week programme at Physical Research Laboratory, Ahmedabad in Space, Atmospheric & Planetary Sciences, Theoretical Physics; minimum 60% aggregate required.

UG/PG

JNCASR Summer Research Fellowship (SRFP)

Two-month summer placement at JNCASR Bengaluru in Physical, Chemical and Life Sciences; for BSc/MSc students with strong academic record.

UG/PG

NCRA–TIFR Visiting Students Programme

Summer research at National Centre for Radio Astrophysics, Pune in Radio Astronomy and Astrophysics; stipend + accommodation for selected students.

UG/PG

IISER Summer Student Programme

Competitive summer research across IISERs in all science disciplines; excellent exposure to frontier research culture; multiple IISER campuses accept applications.

UG/PG

IUAC Training & Research Visitors Programme

Inter-University Accelerator Centre, New Delhi: short-term research visits and accelerator-based experiment training; open to PhD students, postdocs, and faculty.

PhDPostdocFaculty

ISRO YUVIKA — Young Scientist Programme

Annual residential programme at ISRO centres for Class 9 students; covers space science, rocketry, remote sensing, robotics; fully sponsored including travel and accommodation.

School

Lindau Nobel Laureate Meetings

Annual meetings in Germany; highly competitive selection by DST/SERB; connects young researchers with Nobel Laureates. Physics meetings held every 3 years (interleaved with Chemistry & Medicine).

PhDPostdocInternational

ICTP Schools & Research Visits (Trieste)

Abdus Salam ICTP, Trieste — workshops, schools & associate programmes for physicists from developing countries; fully funded; exceptional for condensed matter, optics, and high energy.

PhDPostdocFacultyInternational

Fulbright–Nehru Postdoctoral Fellowship (USA)

8–24 month research fellowship at a US institution for Indian early-career faculty; covers living allowance, J-1 visa, travel and professional allowance. Prestigious government exchange.

PostdocFacultyInternational

Fulbright–Nehru Doctoral Research Fellowship

6–9 month fellowship at a US university for Indian PhD students to carry out part of their doctoral research; managed by USIEF India.

PhDInternational

DAAD Scholarships — Study & Research in Germany

Multiple DAAD programmes for Indian students and researchers: Research Grants for PhD/postdoc (2–12 months in Germany), RISE Worldwide for undergrads, bi-national PhD programmes.

Learning, Courses & Educational Resources
YouTube channels, MOOC platforms, open courseware, textbooks & preprint libraries for Physics

Veritasium

Deep-dive science videos by Dr. Derek Muller — physics, maths, engineering — exceptional production with rigorous content; from quantum mechanics to real-world phenomena.

Free

MinutePhysics

Short animated explanations of physics concepts — from classical mechanics to quantum field theory — in under five minutes. Perfect for building intuition quickly.

Free

3Blue1Brown

Visual mathematics and physics — linear algebra, calculus, Fourier transforms, neural networks — with beautiful animated geometric intuition. Essential for any physicist.

Free

Fermilab (Don Lincoln)

Particle physics, cosmology, and quantum mechanics explained by FNAL physicist Dr. Don Lincoln; 100+ videos on Higgs boson, dark matter, Standard Model and beyond.

Free

PBS Space Time

Graduate-level astrophysics and cosmology — dark matter, general relativity, quantum gravity, black holes — explained rigorously yet accessibly. For serious students.

Free

Sabine Hossenfelder

Theoretical physicist offering clear, critical-minded takes on quantum mechanics, foundations of physics, dark energy, and science news. Cuts through hype with precision.

Free

Physics Girl

Experiment-driven demos of physical phenomena; ideal for undergraduates and school students looking to develop intuition for physics through hands-on exploration.

FreeSchool

Government of India free online courses from IITs, IIMs and Central Universities; 2,000+ courses in Physics, Engineering, Sciences; certifiable credits transferable to degrees.

National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning: full-semester video courses from IIT faculty in all branches of Physics, Electronics and Engineering. Certificate exams available.

Full lecture notes, problem sets and exams from MIT physics courses — Classical Mechanics, E&M, Quantum Mechanics, Statistical Physics through QFT. A gold standard resource.

Research & Laboratory Software
Tools for data acquisition, analysis, crystal structure, DFT, drawing, and scientific writing — free and commercial

LabVIEW (NI)

Industry-standard graphical programming for instrument control, DAQ, and automated test systems; widely used in experimental physics labs worldwide. Student edition available.

Commercial

OriginPro (OriginLab)

Scientific graphing and data analysis: curve fitting, statistical analysis, signal processing, publication-quality figures. The standard tool for experimental physics papers.

Commercial

MATLAB (MathWorks)

Numerical computing, matrix operations, simulation, and signal processing; ubiquitous in experimental and computational physics. Academic licensing available for institutions.

Commercial

Python (NumPy / SciPy / Matplotlib)

Free, open-source; rapidly becoming the standard language for data analysis in experimental physics. Install Anaconda for a complete scientific computing environment in one step.

Free

VESTA

Free 3D crystal structure visualisation; reads VASP, CIF, WIEN2k and many formats; simulates electron density and diffraction patterns. Essential for materials characterisation.

Free

FullProf Suite (Rietveld Refinement)

Free standard software for Rietveld structural refinement of XRD and neutron powder diffraction data; developed at ILL, Grenoble; widely used in condensed matter physics.

Free

JANA2020

Free crystallographic software for Rietveld refinement and structure solution from powder and single-crystal diffraction; handles modulated and composite structures.

Free

CrystalMaker

Powerful crystal and molecular structure visualiser; simulates powder and single-crystal diffraction; excellent for teaching and presentations. Mac/Windows; academic license available.

Commercial

Quantum ESPRESSO (DFT)

Open-source DFT code for electronic structure, band structure, phonon calculations and materials modelling; widely used for condensed matter theory and first-principles research.

Free / Open Source

WIEN2k (All-Electron DFT)

Augmented plane-wave DFT code for electronic, magnetic and optical properties; highly accurate full-potential method; affordable academic licence widely used in India.

Licensed (academic)

VASP (Vienna Ab initio Simulation Package)

Industry-standard plane-wave DFT code; ab initio molecular dynamics, geometry optimisation, band structures; most widely cited computational materials science software.

Commercial

ACD/ChemSketch (Free)

Draw chemical and molecular structures, organometallics, polymers; calculate molecular properties; free for academic non-commercial use. Windows only. Widely used in teaching.

Free (academic)

Avogadro (Molecule Editor)

Free, open-source advanced molecular editor and visualiser for computational chemistry, molecular modelling and materials science. Cross-platform (Windows, macOS, Linux).

Free / Open Source

AutoCAD (Autodesk Education)

Free for verified students and educators through Autodesk Education Community; essential for designing experimental setups, vacuum chambers, cryostat parts, and lab schematics.

Free (edu)

Overleaf — Online LaTeX Editor

Browser-based LaTeX editor with real-time collaboration; free tier available; used by virtually all journals and conference submissions; pre-loaded journal templates.

Free (basic)

Zotero — Reference Manager

Free, open-source reference manager with browser plugin for one-click paper capture from any journal; integrates seamlessly with Word, LibreOffice, and LaTeX/BibTeX.

Free

Mendeley — Reference Manager

Elsevier's free desktop and web reference manager with PDF annotation, social networking for researchers, and Word/BibTeX plug-in; 2 GB free cloud storage.

Free

Cambridge Structural Database (CSD)

World repository for organic & metal-organic crystal structures (~1.2 million entries); CSD Web search is freely accessible for browsing and downloading structures in CIF format.

Free (web)

ICSD — Inorganic Crystal Structure Database

Comprehensive database of inorganic crystal structures (160,000+ entries); covers oxides, nitrides, semiconductors, intermetallics. Institutional subscription required for full access.

Institutional

ICDD Powder Diffraction File (PDF)

The gold-standard XRD reference database (1.1 million+ entries, Release 2026) for phase identification in materials science; supplied with most diffractometer software suites.

Licensed
